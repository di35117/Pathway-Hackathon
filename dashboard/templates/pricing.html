FAQ

Common Questions

How does the Savings-Share Model work? ▾
When our system detects a temperature breach and successfully diverts a truck to prevent spoilage, we calculate the value of cargo saved. LiveCold takes a 5% fee of the verified saved value. You only pay when we demonstrably save your cargo — perfect alignment of incentives.
What are Carbon Credits and how do I earn them? ▾
When LiveCold diverts your truck and prevents food/medicine waste, the CO₂ that would have been emitted by producing replacement goods is avoided. We certify this avoidance as tradeable carbon credits at ₹1,000 per tonne CO₂. Our platform handles the certification, tracking, and facilitation.
Can I switch between plans? ▾
Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. When upgrading, your new tier's features activate immediately. Enterprise customers can also negotiate custom savings-share percentages and carbon credit terms.
What hardware/sensors do I need? ▾
LiveCold works with standard IoT temperature sensors and GPS trackers. We support integration via MQTT protocol. If you already have sensors, we can often integrate with your existing fleet — no additional hardware purchase required for most setups.
How does the Pathway AI pipeline work? ▾
Our pipeline uses Pathway's real-time data processing framework for streaming anomaly detection (4 layers: bounds check, rate-of-change, Z-score statistical, and stuck-sensor detection) combined with a decision engine using sigmoid risk models and cost optimization. SOPs are loaded into a RAG pipeline for AI-powered recommendations.
